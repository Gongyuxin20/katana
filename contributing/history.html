This repository began life as a fork the Galois project. As part of the commercialization process, this repository was combined with proprietary parts of the Katana system and later disentangled. Disentangling the open-source and proprietary components required rewriting the git history of this repository. Because of this, commits from 2020-06-30 until 2020-10-29 (marked with [REWRITTEN COMMIT: incomplete subtree]) are unbuildable and inconsistent; they are included to provide context only. Commits from 2020-10-29 until 2020-01-01 are consistent (marked with [REWRITTEN COMMIT: self-contained subtree]), but may have removed changes mentioned in the commit messages.
